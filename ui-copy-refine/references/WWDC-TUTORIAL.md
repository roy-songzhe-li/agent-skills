**来源:** Bilibili - Apple开发者

## 4个核心原则

### 1. Remove Fillers（移除填充词）

删除不必要的副词、形容词、感叹词和客套话。

**填充词类型：**
- 副词：easily, quickly, simply
- 形容词：fast, simple, great
- 感叹词：Uh oh, Oops, Hooray
- 客套话：Sorry, Thank you, Please

**示例：**
- ❌ "Simply enter your license plate number to quickly pay for parking"
- ✅ "Enter your license plate number to pay for parking"

**何时保留描述性词语：**
- ✅ "**Automatically** feed your pets when you're away"
  → "Automatically" 阐明了关键功能

---

### 2. Avoid Repetition（避免重复）

不要用不同方式重复同一信息。抵制填充空白空间的冲动。

**示例：快递延迟提醒**

**优化前：**
- 标题: "We're running late"
- 正文: "Your delivery driver won't make it on time. They'll be there in 10 minutes."

**优化后：**
- 标题: "Delivery delayed 10 minutes"
- 正文: "Check the app for your driver's location"

---

### 3. Lead with the Why（优先说明原因）

将好处/原因放在句子开头。

**公式:** To [好处], do [行动]

**示例：**
- ❌ "Solve today's crossword to keep your streak going"
- ✅ "Keep your streak going by solving today's crossword"

- ❌ "Enter your phone number to get reservation updates"
- ✅ "To get reservation updates, enter your phone number"

---

### 4. Make a Word List（建立词汇表）

保持术语一致性。

**表格示例（游戏 App）：**

| 使用 | 避免 | 定义 |
|------|------|------|
| Alias | Handle, Username, Title | 玩家游戏内名称 |
| Health | Lives, Hearts, Energy | 玩家生存能力 |
| Find | Search, Look for | 查找操作 |

## 实战案例：AirPods Pro 听力测试

Apple 将4个原则应用在实际产品中：

**标题:** "Test your hearing"
- ✅ 无填充词

**正文1:** "Hearing loss is common and can get worse over time"
- ✅ 优先说明原因

**正文2:** "If you have mild or moderate hearing loss, AirPods Pro can provide hearing assistance"
- ✅ 再次说明好处

**按钮:** "Next"
- ✅ 相同操作使用相同名称

**指导屏幕:**
- 标题: "Find a quiet place where you can focus and take the test"
- 说明: "Too much background noise can cause inaccurate results"
- ✅ 标题给指示，说明补充新信息，无重复

## 金句

> "UX writing is all about economy of language."

> "Does each word add value?"

> "Read your writing out loud. It makes it easier to hear those filler and repetitive words."
